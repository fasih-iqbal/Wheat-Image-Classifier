About Wheat Classification

Why Classify?

Wheat classification helps determine quality, market value, and best uses for different varieties. Proper identification ensures optimal processing and application.

How It Works

Our AI model analyzes visual characteristics of wheat spikes to identify their specific category. The system uses advanced computer vision to detect subtle differences between varieties.

Accuracy

The classification model has been trained on 3000 plus wheat images for high accuracy results. Our system achieves over 99% accuracy across all major wheat categories.
